Why you should get a gaming pc

Number 1

Now, the first reason why you should get a pc is because pc's are really reliable and they last a long time. I have had my gaming pc for almost 4 years now. It is still a very good gaming pc but there is some things that I would like to upgrade like the ram, motherboard, graphics card, and the cpu. Most PC's last for 5-8 years before having big problems.

Number 2

Now you may ask "Why should I get a pc, they are so expensive!I may as well get a xbox or a ps4" Well you should get a pc because you can do a lot more things than Xbox's or Ps4's.

Number 3

You can plug a controller into your pc and have way better performance because consoles only have 60fps rather than the 100+ fps that computers have.

Number 4

If you have a console your max hz that you can have on your monitor or tv is 60. Pc's instead can have 240hz which makes your game way smoother and the graphics better.
